Area

With its network of footpaths and cycle routes, Morbihan has plenty of options for those who enjoy exploring the countryside at a gentler pace. At the house you’ll find books and maps to help you plan your day. We also have two bikes for people to use.

As the nearest beach is only 45 minutes drive away, it’s easy to spend the day by the sea. The coast of Brittany has so many beautiful resorts and isolated coves to discover - whether you enjoy swimming, sun-bathing, or surfing, you'll soon find your favourite beach!

Brittany also has a rich cultural heritage to discover, evident in its standing stones, ornate churches and love of all things Celtic.
